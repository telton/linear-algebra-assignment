Explanation of the Data

The response variable y is the IMDB Rating of the movies in the dataset. The _predictor_ variable _x_ is the body count is the body count that the movie produces. The dataset was compiled using data from the website Movie Body Counts. To be counted a kill and/or dead body has to be visible on the screen, implied deaths like those died in the explosion of the Death Star are not counted. For more details on how counts should be conducted see their guidelines at http://moviebodycounts.proboards.com/thread/6.
